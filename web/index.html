2. Field-Table Grid Convergence (M2)

In idealized mode, the radial electric field (1/r) and longitudinal magnetic field (constant Bz) are evaluated using infinite-precision analytical continuous functions. For self-consistent space-charge-limited (SCL) flow (Track B / M5), grid resolution studies show a 128 × 128 (r, z) cylindrical grid resolves potential gradients in the thin cathode emission sheath layer.
